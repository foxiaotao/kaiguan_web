How long have you been learning English? 你学英语有多久了?

It has been ...

 It’s been years. 好多年了。

It’s been a long time. 挺久了/挺长时间了（没见面了）。

It’s been too long. 太久了/太长时间了（没见面）。

It’s been two days. 已经两天了（我们来多久了）。

It’s been three months. 已经三个月了（这种状况）。

It’s only been three years. 只有三年（我们认识）。

It’s been half an hour and you still have not said one word. 已经半个小时了而你依然没有开口说话。

It has been one year since we met. 我们认识已经一年了。

It has been three years since I left. 我离开（家乡）已经三年了。

before ...

 Ten days before Christmas. 离圣诞节还有十天。
