• 常用的表达：daytime、nighttime等

【名词形式的表达】

                 

daytime  [英][ˈdeɪtaɪm] [美][ˈdeˌtaɪm]

He stayed home in the daytime. 他白天待在家里。

I couldn’t sleep at night and I was getting so tired in the daytime. 我晚上睡不着而在白天又觉得很累（恶性循环）。

especially during the daytime，尤其是在白天（老是打瞌睡）。

It is the daytime. 现在是白天。

It looks like the daytime. 像白天一样。

day  [英][deɪ] [美][de]

I’m up all night, I’m dozing off during the day. 我夜里睡不着（失眠），白天则一直打瞌睡。

nighttime  [英]['naɪttaɪm] [美]['naɪtˌtaɪm]

at nighttime，在夜间。

night  [英][naɪt] [美][naɪt]

at night，在晚上。

It is night. 现在是晚上。

【形容词/副词形式的表达】               

daytime

 daytime temperatures，白天的温度。
